A Note on Privacy

This website was built as a private, space to preserve family memories securely, away from public platforms like YouTube, Facebook, or Google Photos.

The site itself is statically generated by me, and hosted through GitHub Pages, a service operated by GitHub, a global software platform trusted by millions of developers and organizations worldwide. GitHub Pages delivers content over HTTPS using strong TLS encryption, ensuring all pages are served safely and cannot be altered or intercepted in transit.

All video content is delivered through Cloudflare Stream, a professional-grade, encrypted video delivery platform built by Cloudflare, one of the world’s leading internet security and performance providers. Cloudflare protects websites for governments, businesses, and independent creators alike, their network automatically encrypts data, mitigates DDoS attacks, and prevents unauthorized access or indexing.

No analytics, cookies, or tracking pixels are used. The site does not share data with advertisers, social networks, or external APIs. Every photo and video remains private, unlisted, and accessible only via direct link, fully under our control.
