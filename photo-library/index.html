A Note on Privacy

This website was built as a private, space to preserve family memories securely, away from public platforms like YouTube, Facebook, or Google Photos.

GitHub Pages delivers content over HTTPS using strong TLS encryption, ensuring all pages are served safely and cannot be altered or intercepted in transit.
